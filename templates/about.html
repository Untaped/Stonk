INVESTING STRATEGY RECOMMENDATION
The strategy I found most successful is to take the top 10 recommendations — as long as they have high confidence from both the 5-day and 30-day lists — narrow the list down through research, and invest. Check the news daily for qualitative factors and revisit the site to see if the algorithm's confidence in those stocks changes. If confidence drops, sell. If it holds, hold. As you exit positions, rotate into new high-confidence picks.
