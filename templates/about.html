Investing Strategy Recommendation

The strategy that I found most successful is to take the top 10 recommendations—as long as they have high confidence levels—from both the 5- and 30-day lists, narrow that list down through research to 5–10 stocks, and invest. Every day, check the website to see if the algorithm's confidence in those specific stocks changes. If that confidence is low, sell, and if that confidence is high, hold. As you sell, continue to find the top recommendations with high confidence levels to refill your portfolio.
